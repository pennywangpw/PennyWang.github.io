About Me...

Hello! I'm Penny, a software engineer with global B2B and B2C customer success and sales experience. These experiences have helped me develop strong listening skills and the ability to quickly understand people’s needs.
Throughout my career, I benefited from using a variety of software tools as a user.
Imagine managing a large to-do list without relying on post-it, or sifting through thousands of files to generate a detailed analysis report. Or think about helping people of all generations easily make appointments with their doctors or reservations for a trip when using a website for the first time.....
To me, the beauty of increasing efficiency and simplifying complex processes inspired me to become a developer.
To pursue this passion, I joined an online bootcamp, where I learned JavaScript, Python, React, Redux, Express, Flask, HTML/CSS, and SQL. I gained valuable experience working both independently and collaboratively, contributing to various projects by utilizing the agile methodology.
I truly enjoy learning by doing, and I continue to collaborate on projects with others. I'm enthusiastic about exploring opportunities where I can leverage my background in software engineering to solve people’s pain points and make their lives easier, just like I did before.
